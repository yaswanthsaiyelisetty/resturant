Welcome to Eat Heat

A Legacy of Andhra Flavors & Bamboo Elegance

Located in the vibrant town of Narasaraopet, Andhra Pradesh, Eat Heat — The Bamboo Restaurant stands as a tribute to the rich culinary traditions of the Telugu heartland. Inspired by the lush bamboo groves that dot the Andhra countryside, our restaurant merges rustic natural beauty with modern fine-dining sophistication.

Since our founding in 2016, we have been on a relentless pursuit of culinary excellence. Our head chef, Rajan Reddy, brings over two decades of expertise in Andhra cuisine, drawing from family recipes passed down through generations. Every dish on our menu tells the story of Narasaraopet — from the fiery Gongura Chicken that embodies Andhra's love for bold flavors to the aromatic Bamboo Biryani that is our signature creation.

The bamboo is not just in our name — it's woven into every element of our space. Hand-crafted bamboo installations, naturally lit dining areas, and eco-conscious design create an atmosphere that is both intimate and awe-inspiring. Whether you're here for a family celebration, a romantic dinner, or a casual meal with friends, every visit to Eat Heat is a journey of the senses.

Chef Rajan Reddy

Chef's Philosophy

"Food is fire, family, and memory — every plate we serve carries the soul of Andhra."

Our kitchen operates on a simple belief: the finest flavors come from the freshest ingredients, time-honored techniques, and an unyielding passion for perfection. We source our spices from local Andhra farms and our vegetables from morning markets.

Our Journey

Milestones

2016

The Beginning

Eat Heat opens its doors in Narasaraopet with a small 20-seat restaurant and a bold vision to bring premium Andhra dining to the town.

2018

Bamboo Transformation

Major renovation introduces our signature bamboo-themed interiors, transforming the space into an immersive dining experience.

2020

Award-Winning Biryani

Our Special Bamboo Biryani wins "Best Biryani in Andhra Pradesh" at the Regional Food Awards, putting Eat Heat on the culinary map.

2022

Expansion

Restaurant expands to 80 seats, adds private dining rooms, and launches catering services for weddings and corporate events.

2025

10,000+ Happy Guests

We proudly serve our 10,000th guest, earning 4.8-star ratings across Google and Zomato with hundreds of glowing reviews.
