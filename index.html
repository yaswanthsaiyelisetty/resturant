Our Story

A Culinary Journey Through Andhra Pradesh

Nestled in the heart of Narasaraopet, Eat Heat — The Bamboo Restaurant is more than just a dining destination. It is a celebration of Andhra Pradesh's rich culinary heritage, wrapped in the organic elegance of bamboo architecture. Every corner of our restaurant tells a story — from the rustic bamboo groves that inspired our interiors to the time-honored family recipes that define our kitchen.

Our menu is a love letter to the fiery flavors of Andhra cuisine. Each dish is crafted from recipes passed down through generations, using the freshest local ingredients and authentic spice blends that set Andhra food apart from any other. From our signature slow-cooked biryanis to the sizzling pulkas hot off the tandoor, every bite is a journey through the land of spice.

Founded by a family passionate about sharing their heritage with the world, Eat Heat bridges the gap between rustic tradition and modern sophistication — a place where bamboo elegance meets Andhra heat.

Chef Rajan Reddy
